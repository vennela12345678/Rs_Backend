HOW IT WORKS

From Farm to Table,
Stay Residue-Free

1

Log Animal ID

Simply enter the unique tag number or animal ID into the secure text field to instantly access or create a health profile.

2

Record Treatment Details

Input the specific medication, dosage, and date. The system is designed to handle complex Antimicrobial Usage (AMU) data with ease.

3

Automated Safety Calculation

ResidueSafe’s logic engine instantly calculates the exact Withdrawal Period required to meet Maximum Residue Limit (MRL) safety standards.

4

Monitor Real-Time Status

Track progress through a visual dashboard. Live countdowns categorize every animal as Compliant, Under Treatment, or Warning.

5

Verify Market Readiness

Confirm that the withdrawal period has successfully ended, ensuring that every animal is 100% residue-free and safe for the food supply chain.
