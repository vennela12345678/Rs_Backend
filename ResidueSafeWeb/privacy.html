Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, register an animal, or record a treatment. This may include your name, email address, farm details, and livestock data. This information is essential for providing the clinical tracking services ResidueSafe offers.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information to provide, maintain, and improve our services, including calculating withdrawal periods and ensuring food safety compliance. Your data helps us refine our algorithms and ensure that every livestock record is accurate and compliant with global safety standards.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. We employ industry-standard encryption and security protocols to ensure that your private farm and livestock data remains secure at all times.
