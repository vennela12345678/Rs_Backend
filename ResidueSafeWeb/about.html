About ResidueSafe

Overview

About the Platform

ResidueSafe simplifies livestock treatment monitoring to ensure strict compliance with food safety standards. We empower farmers and administrators with a transparent, digital audit trail for safe, residue-free production. By bridging the gap between clinical treatment and consumer protection, we ensure that every product entering the food chain is of the highest safety standard.

Core Capabilities

Key Features

MRL Compliance Monitoring

Ensure all livestock meet strict Maximum Residue Limits before entering the food chain.

Real-Time AMU Tracking

Maintain a digital record of all antimicrobial usage and drug administrations for every animal.

Automated Withdrawal Countdowns

Calculate and display precise Meat, Milk, and Egg withdrawal periods automatically.

Smart Animal Identification

Instantly register and identify animals by scanning ear tags using integrated OCR technology.

Global Safety Compliance

Ensuring all treatments follow international food safety and veterinary guidelines.

Digital Traceability

Complete, unalterable history for every animal to satisfy safety audits and inspections.

Data Sovereignty

Your farm data is encrypted and completely under your control, accessible anytime, anywhere.

Multi-Species Specialized

Customized safety parameters for Cows, Buffalo, Hens, Goats, and Sheep.
